BURJ KHALIFA


The Burj Khalifa, located in Dubai, UAE, is the tallest building in the world, standing at a height of 828 meters (2,717 feet). Completed in 2010, it is an iconic symbol of Dubai's ambition and architectural innovation. Designed by the American firm Skidmore, Owings & Merrill, the Burj Khalifa combines Islamic architectural elements with cutting-edge technology.It houses luxury apartments, corporate offices, the Armani Hotel, and observation decks that offer breathtaking views of Dubai's skyline. The building is surrounded by the Dubai Fountain, one of the world's largest choreographed fountain systems. During special events, such as New Year's Eve or major celebrations, the Burj Khalifa becomes a canvas for dazzling light shows and fireworks, cementing its status as a global landmark.
