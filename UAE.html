UAE

UAE: Capital: Abu Dhabi (the capital), with Dubai being the largest city and a global financial hub

Language: Arabic (official), English widely spoken

Currency: Emirati Dirham (AED)

Government: Federation of seven emirates, each ruled by its own monarch, with the President typically from Abu Dhabi

Economy: Diversified beyond oil, with strong sectors in tourism, aviation, real estate, and finance

Known for: Iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa in Dubai, hosting major international events like Expo 2020, and a reputation as a business and tourism hub.
