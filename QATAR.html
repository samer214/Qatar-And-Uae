QATAR

Qatar: Capital: Doha

Language: Arabic (official), English widely spoken

Currency: Qatari Riyal (QAR)

Government: Absolute monarchy, ruled by the Emir of Qatar

Economy: Majorly driven by natural gas and oil, making it one of the richest countries per capita

Known for: Hosting major international events, including the 2022 FIFA World Cup, its luxury lifestyle, and its significant investment in global infrastructure
